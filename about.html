About

Alundra 2: A New Legend Begins, is an action role-playing game developed by Matrix Software for PlayStation 1. The game was first released on November 18, 1999 in Japan. later in 2000, the game was release in USA and Canada (March) and EU (June).

At the moment, the game is available in 5 languages, with 4 official translations and 1 unofficial translations.

- アランドラ2 魔進化の謎

- Alundra 2: A New Legend Begins

- Alundra 2: Der Beginn einer neuen Legende
