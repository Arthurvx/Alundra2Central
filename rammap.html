Alundra 2 RAM MAP

This RAM MAP is useful if you want to hack, change, test in-game functions or even create Lua Scripts.

Version used is NTSC-U [SLUS-01017], therefore those address may be different from another regions, such as NTSC-J or PAL.

At the moment, this list has a total of 12 addresses.
